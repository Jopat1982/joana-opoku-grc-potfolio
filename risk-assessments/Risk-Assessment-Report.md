# Cybersecurity Risk Assessment Report

## Company
ABC Manufacturing

## Assessment Date
July 2026

## Objective
Evaluate cybersecurity risks affecting business operations and recommend security controls to reduce organizational risk.

## Scope
This assessment covers:
- Corporate network
- Employee workstations
- Cloud services
- Email systems
- Third-party vendors

## Risk Summary

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Risk Level |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Phishing Attack | High | High | Critical |
| Weak Passwords | Medium | High | High |
| Unpatched Systems | High | High | Critical |
| Vendor Data Breach | Medium | High | High |
| Lost Laptop | Low | Medium | Medium |

## Recommendations

- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Perform monthly vulnerability scans
- Conduct annual security awareness training
- Encrypt company laptops
- Review vendor security annually

## Framework

This assessment follows the NIST Risk Management Framework (RMF) and references NIST SP 800-53 security controls.

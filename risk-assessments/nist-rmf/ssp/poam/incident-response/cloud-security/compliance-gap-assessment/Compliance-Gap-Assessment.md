# Compliance Gap Assessment

## Assessment Overview

**Framework:** NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5

**Organization:** ABC Manufacturing

**Assessment Date:** July 2026

---

## Executive Summary

A compliance assessment was performed to determine alignment with NIST SP 800-53 security controls. Most controls were implemented successfully; however, several gaps requiring remediation were identified.

---

## Gap Assessment

| Control | Requirement | Current State | Gap | Recommendation | Priority |
|---------|-------------|---------------|-----|----------------|----------|
| AC-2 | Account Management | Manual reviews | No quarterly review | Automate access reviews | High |
| IA-2 | Multi-Factor Authentication | Enabled for admins | Users excluded | Enable MFA for all users | Critical |
| AU-6 | Audit Review | Weekly review | No documented process | Create audit review procedure | Medium |
| SI-2 | Patch Management | Monthly patching | Servers delayed | Patch within 30 days | High |
| CP-9 | Backup | Daily backups | Recovery not tested | Perform quarterly recovery tests | Medium |

---

## Overall Compliance

Overall Compliance Score: **88%**

---

## Recommendations

- Expand Multi-Factor Authentication
- Formalize audit review procedures
- Improve patch management timelines
- Conduct quarterly disaster recovery testing
- Perform continuous compliance monitoring

---

## Conclusion

The organization demonstrates a mature security posture with several opportunities for improvement. Addressing the identified gaps will strengthen compliance with NIST SP 800-53 and reduce organizational risk.

## Purpose
This POA&M identifies known security weaknesses, planned corrective actions, responsible personnel, and expected completion dates.

| Weakness ID | Weakness | Risk Level | Corrective Action | Owner | Target Date | Status |
|--------------|-----------|------------|-------------------|-------|-------------|--------|
| POAM-001 | Multi-Factor Authentication not enabled for privileged accounts | High | Implement MFA for all administrator accounts | IT Security | 2026-08-15 | Open |
| POAM-002 | Critical operating system patches missing | High | Apply vendor patches and verify installation | Infrastructure Team | 2026-07-30 | In Progress |
| POAM-003 | Vendor security assessment overdue | Medium | Perform annual third-party risk assessment | Compliance Team | 2026-08-10 | Open |
| POAM-004 | Backup restoration not tested | Medium | Conduct disaster recovery testing | Infrastructure Team | 2026-08-20 | Planned |
| POAM-005 | Security awareness training incomplete | Low | Require all employees to complete annual training | Human Resources | 2026-07-25 | In Progress |

## Summary

The Information Security Office reviews the POA&M monthly to ensure remediation activities remain on schedule. High-risk findings receive priority until remediation is complete.

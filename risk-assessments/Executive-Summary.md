# Executive Summary

## Company

ABC Manufacturing Corporation

## Assessment Purpose

The purpose of this cybersecurity risk assessment was to identify security risks that could affect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of company information systems.

## Assessment Scope

The assessment included:

- Corporate network
- Employee laptops
- Microsoft 365 environment
- Cloud infrastructure
- Third-party vendors
- Remote employees

## Overall Risk Rating

**High**

Several critical risks were identified that require immediate attention.

## Top Risks

1. Phishing attacks
2. Weak password practices
3. Unpatched systems
4. Vendor security risks
5. Unauthorized access

## Recommendations

- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Enforce strong password policies
- Perform monthly vulnerability scans
- Conduct annual vendor security reviews
- Provide security awareness training

## Conclusion

Addressing these risks will improve the organization's security posture and reduce the likelihood of successful cyberattacks.

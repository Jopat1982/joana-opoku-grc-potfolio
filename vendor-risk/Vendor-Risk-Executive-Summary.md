# Vendor Risk Executive Summary

## Organization
ABC Manufacturing Corporation

## Vendor
CloudPayroll Services

## Assessment Purpose

This assessment evaluated the cybersecurity posture of CloudPayroll Services before granting access to company systems and sensitive employee payroll data.

## Assessment Scope

- Data Protection
- Access Management
- Multi-Factor Authentication
- Incident Response
- Business Continuity
- Compliance
- Vendor Security Controls

## Overall Risk Rating

**High**

## Key Findings

- Multi-factor authentication was not enforced for all users.
- Annual penetration testing documentation was unavailable.
- Security awareness training records were incomplete.
- Incident response procedures required improvement.

## Recommendation

Proceed with the vendor only after the identified security gaps have been remediated and validated.
